Our Story

Beats That Build Futures

Founded in 2015 in Busabala, Kampala, Rwambela Drumline uses traditional Ugandan drumming to create opportunities for vulnerable youth.

Musical Training

We teach professional drumming and performance skills to at-risk youth

Education Support

Performance earnings fund school fees and vocational training

Safe Shelter

We provide dormitories and meals for our drumline members
