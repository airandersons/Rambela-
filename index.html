About Rambela

Our Mission

To provide underprivileged children with access to quality education, safe accommodation, and proper nutrition, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their background, has equal opportunities to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Empowerment, and Community. We believe in transparency and accountability in all our operations.

Our Programs

We run several initiatives to support children in need

Education

Providing school fees, uniforms, books, and mentorship to ensure children receive quality education.

Food & Nutrition

Daily meals and nutrition programs to combat malnutrition and support healthy development.

Accommodation

Safe shelters for homeless children, providing a stable environment for growth.

Orphan Support

Comprehensive care including counseling, healthcare, and family placement services.
