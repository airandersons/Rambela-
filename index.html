Who We Are

About Rambela

Rambela is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children in urban slums and rural communities.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, safe shelter, and proper nutrition to children in need, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty.

Our Vision

A world where every child has equal opportunities to thrive regardless of their background or circumstances.

What We Do

Our Programs

We run comprehensive programs to address the needs of vulnerable children

Education Support

We provide school fees, uniforms, books, and mentorship programs to ensure children receive quality education and have the tools to succeed.

Learn More

Food & Nutrition

Our nutrition programs provide daily meals and education about healthy eating to combat malnutrition and support proper development.

Learn More

Safe Accommodation

We build and maintain shelters that provide a safe, stable environment for homeless children, offering more than just a roof over their heads.

Learn More

Orphan Support

Comprehensive care including counseling, healthcare, education, and family placement services for orphaned children.
