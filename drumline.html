Our Sound

Traditional Instruments

Authentic Ugandan drums and percussion

Engalabi

Traditional long drum from Uganda, played with hands. Produces deep, resonant bass tones that form the foundation of our rhythms.

Entenga

Set of tuned drums played by multiple drummers. Creates melodic patterns that complement the main rhythm section.

Djembe

West African drum now integral to Ugandan music. Provides sharp slaps and clear tones for rhythmic accents.

Adungu

Traditional bow harp from Northern Uganda. Adds melodic and harmonic layers to our performances.

Endingidi

Tube fiddle with one string, played with a bow. Provides unique timbres and melodic lines in our arrangements.

Percussion

Shakers, bells, and rattles that add texture and complexity to our rhythmic patterns.
